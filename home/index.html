Why Gadget Salvation?

We’re obsessive about tech

With a database of over 4,000 laptops, smart phones and more, it’s simple to find your gadget. Once you select your gadget, our custom algorithm pulls pricing data from eBay, Amazon and Rakuten to calculate your estimate, making sure you get a fair price, and without a doubt the best place to sell laptop for cash.

We make it easy

Once you accept your estimate, all you have to do is print out the prepaid shipping label, drop off your package and wait for payment, which you’ll be sent within two business days of us receiving your gadget. And there’s no need to worry about your data––we reset your gadget to the factory settings as part of the testing process.

We care about our customers and the planet

We began Gadget Salvation back in 2008 to give used electronics a new life. Since then, we’ve saved thousands of computers, laptops, phones, tablets and more from landfills, and paid our customers in the process.
